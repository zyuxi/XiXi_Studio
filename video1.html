Bored waiting for the bus?
Want to see something while in the toilet?
Want to take a short break but have no idea what to do?
Want to take a few minutes to get some news about the world?

Random Minutes is an app for browsing fragmented life information.
Here, you can browse various types of information such as pictures, videos, news, jokes, game information, and sports broadcasts.
Based on your preferences, we will provide you with information that you may interested in.
