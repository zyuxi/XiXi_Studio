Poster Design
Chinese Twenty-Four Solar Terms in Midjourney
DESIGNER | YUXI ZHANG

I've created a set of posters for the traditional Chinese Twenty-Four Solar Terms, inspired by the style of Hayao Miyazaki, realised through the artificial intelligence tool Midjourney. These posters blend Miyazaki's fantastical aesthetics with the profound cultural essence of the Chinese solar terms, aiming to showcase the wonderful interplay between the cycles of nature and cultural harmony. Each piece is a tribute to nature and an exploration of traditional wisdom, inviting viewers to experience the rhythm of the changing seasons and the cultural significance behind each solar term.
